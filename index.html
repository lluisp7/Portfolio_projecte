Elon Must Survive

Description

"Elon Must Survive" is an action-survival game where Elon Musk must cross a highway full of out-of-control Tesla cars, driven by a rogue AI. The player must guide Elon to dodge the vehicles and reach the Tesla factory to shut down the AI.

Gameplay

Control Elon Musk using arrow keys or WASD to move up, down, left, and right.

Avoid Tesla cars speeding across multiple lanes to reach the top of the screen and advance to the next level.

Objective

Cars move randomly across lanes and reset with increased speed after each level. If hit, the game ends with a "Game Over" screen, offering a restart option.
